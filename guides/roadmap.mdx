## How to Shape the Roadmap

1. Share ideas via Discord or email `hello@humyn.space`.  
2. Include project context, target personas, and example threads that show the need.  
3. Upvote existing requests—community signal influences prioritization.

We review feedback every week and update this page after each planning cycle.
