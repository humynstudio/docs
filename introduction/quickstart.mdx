## Launch in five moves

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="1. Sign in or create your account" icon="key">
    Accept the invite or create a workspace and enable 2FA.
  </Card>
  <Card title="2. Create a new project" icon="folder-plus">
    Click `New project`, select an icon, set a clear name, and add a short description so everyone knows the lens you’re watching.
  </Card>
  <Card title="3. Open Add Comments" icon="comment-plus">
    Jump into the <a href="../core-features/add-comments">Add Comments</a> tab to define the research topic and the POV you want Humyn to monitor.
  </Card>
  <Card title="4. Add keywords & Reddit sources" icon="list-check">
    Enter the keywords, phrases, and Reddit URLs that capture your audience so Humyn ingests the right threads. Runs typically process within a few minutes depending on volume.
  </Card>
  <Card title="5. Explore or Chat for insight" icon="sparkles">
    Head to `Explore` for community-wide patterns or `Chat` to ask follow-up questions and unlock takeaways fast.
  </Card>
</Columns>
