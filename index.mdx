## How teams ramp in week one

<Columns cols={3}>
  <Card title="1. Ingest signal" icon="download">
    Connect the first communities, confirm the prompts provided in Quickstart, and validate that the pipeline is capturing the right threads.
  </Card>
  <Card title="2. Frame the story" icon="sparkles">
    Capture the three strongest quotes, tag sentiment, and turn them into a single insight card for your kickoff sync.
  </Card>
  <Card title="3. Share & iterate" icon="share-nodes">
    Publish the recap, gather stakeholder feedback, and adjust prompts or sources before you scale up the intake.
  </Card>
</Columns>
